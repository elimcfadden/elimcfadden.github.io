About Me

My name is Eli McFadden, and I'm an aspiring sports analyst. I will be graduating this December from Utah State University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems with an emphasis in Data Engineering, along with a minor in Data Analytics. I'm passionate about basketball and the NBA, and I enjoy digging into the numbers and using them to drive real discussion and debate.

About Shattered Backboard Analytics

Shattered Backboard Analytics is an NBA-only visualization and opinion site built on analysis. The idea is simple: share takes that might sound bold at first, but shouldn't be once the data is on the table.

Everything here is backed by visualizations I create, with article-style breakdowns that explain what the charts are actually saying. Right now, my visualizations are built in Tableau, and over time I plan to expand into tools like Power BI and deeper analysis using SQL and Python. There isn't a set posting schedule yet, but I'll keep adding new work as the site evolves.
